Create docs/garlic-autumn-plan.md documenting hardneck garlic planting for October 2026 (COOK-02). Standalone document since it serves a future purpose and will be referenced when autumn arrives.

Structure:

1. **Overview:**
   - What: Hardneck garlic cloves planted autumn 2026 for July 2027 harvest
   - Why: 9-month crop cycle, teaches patience and long-term reward, scapes are a bonus harvest in June
   - Child angle: "Plant it, forget it over winter, watch it appear in spring like magic"

2. **Timing for Vejle, Denmark (Zone 7b):**
   - Plant window: Late September to mid-October 2026
   - Why: 4-6 weeks before first hard frost (typically early-to-mid November in Vejle)
   - Too early risk: August/early September causes excessive top growth that winter-kills
   - Too late risk: After late October gives insufficient rooting time

3. **Variety Selection:**
   - Recommended: Hardneck varieties (Music, German Extra Hardy, or Chesnok Red)
   - Source: Seed garlic from garden center or online. Do NOT use supermarket garlic (treated, may carry disease)
   - Danish sources to check at purchase time: local garden centers, online seed suppliers

4. **Bed Assignment:**
   - Primary option: Bed D (terrace, south end) -- spring onions will be finished by October
   - Alternative: Section of Bed C -- after warm-season crops (tomatoes/cucumbers) are removed
   - Note: "Decide at planting time based on which bed has cleared first"

5. **Planting Instructions:**
   - Break bulb into individual cloves (keep papery skin on)
   - Plant 10-15cm deep, pointed end UP, 15cm apart
   - Water in well after planting
   - Mulch immediately: 10-15cm straw over the planted area

6. **Winter Care:** None -- leave under mulch until spring

7. **Spring-Summer 2027 Timeline:**
   - March-April: Green shoots emerge through mulch
   - June: Remove scapes when they curl (cut, don't pull) -- edible bonus harvest
   - July: Harvest when lower 3-4 leaves have browned. Pull, don't dig.
   - July-August: Cure for 2-3 weeks in dry, ventilated shade before storage

8. **Shopping List:**
   - 1 bulb seed garlic (hardneck variety) -- produces 6-10 cloves = 6-10 plants
   - Straw mulch (small bale, or use leftover from bed mulching)

Cross-reference docs/planting-grid-bed-d.md for bed D current layout.
