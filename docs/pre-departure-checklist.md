# Vacation Prep Checklist

**Print this page and tape it to the fridge. Check off each item as you complete it.**

---

## Day -3: Mulching Day

- [ ] Take a photo of each bed (A, B) for the neighbor guide
- [ ] Spread straw mulch on Bed A -- 5-8cm depth, leave space around stems, keep fill tube clear
- [ ] Spread straw mulch on Bed B -- 5-8cm depth, leave space around stems and strawberry crowns
- [ ] Check mulch depth on both beds with the marked stick (5-8cm)
- [ ] Deep water both beds after mulching

## Day -2: Harvest Party

- [ ] Fill Bed A north zone reservoir via fill tube (before child joins)
- [ ] Harvest all ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers from Bed A north zone
- [ ] Harvest all ripe beans from Bed A north zone
- [ ] Harvest all ripe lettuce/herbs from Bed A middle zone
- [ ] Harvest all ripe raspberries from Bed A south end
- [ ] Harvest any ripe broccoli from Bed A middle zone
- [ ] Harvest all ripe strawberries from Bed B
- [ ] Wash, count, and celebrate the harvest together

## Day -1: Neighbor Day

- [ ] Print 2 copies of the neighbor guide (fill in phone number, attach photos)
- [ ] Walk primary neighbor through the garden bed by bed (15-20 min)
- [ ] Hand printed guide to primary neighbor
- [ ] Deliver backup copy to second neighbor
- [ ] Final top-up watering -- both beds
- [ ] Child says goodbye to each bed
