### Nature Corner

The nature corner (log pile, stone pile, insect hotel) is located in the **northeast corner** of the backyard, behind the swing set along the far-right boundary fence. This shaded, sheltered area is ideal for wildlife habitat.

## Sun Exposure Zones

| Zone | Location | Summer Sun | Notes |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| **Full sun** | West-central backyard, roof terrace | 8-10 hours direct | Best growing positions; terrace is above tree canopy |
| **Partial shade** | Transitional area around beds, mid-backyard | 4-6 hours direct | Some filtering from nearby trees, especially afternoon |
| **Shade** | Under mature tree canopy along east and north boundaries | 2-4 hours direct | Not suitable for food crops; good for nature corner |

**Key observations:**
- The house faces **west** toward the street. The backyard extends **east and northeast**.
- Morning sun comes from the east (over the tree canopy). Afternoon sun from the south and west is strongest.
- Backyard beds (A, B, C) are positioned in the north-central lawn to maximize sun while staying clear of the east/north tree canopy.
- The roof terrace receives excellent sun from all directions due to its elevation.
