# My Garden Morning / Min Have-Morgen

**5 minutes -- every morning -- same 5 steps**

---

## The Walk

### 1. LOOK 👀

> Walk past all beds. What changed overnight?

**Bed A** -- **Bed B**

*Far siger: "Gaa forbi alle bedene. Hvad har aendret sig siden i gaar?"*

---

### 2. TOUCH 🤚

> Feel the soil in each bed. Dry = water time!

Stick a finger in the soil. Does it feel dry and dusty, or cool and damp?

*Far siger: "Stik en finger i jorden. Er den toer? Saa skal vi vande!"*

---

### 3. WATER 💧

> Small watering can, gentle pour on dry beds

Only the dry ones! Pour slowly at the base of the plants, not on the leaves.

*Far siger: "Vand forsigtigt ved roden -- ikke paa bladene!"*

---

### 4. PICK 🧺

> Any ripe fruits or veggies? Herbs to smell?

- Bed A: Peas in fat pods, ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, herbs to smell, lettuce to snip, ripe raspberries
- Bed B: Red strawberries (leave the white ones!), radish tops poking up, spring onions to pull

*Far siger: "Pluk kun de RODE jordbaer -- lad de hvide sidde til i morgen!"*

---

### 5. MEASURE 📏

> How tall is the sunflower today? Mark on the ruler stick

Stand next to the ruler stake in Bed B. Where does the sunflower reach today?

*Far siger: "Stil dig ved maalestokken -- hvor naar solsikken hen til i dag?"*

## Father's Notes

- **Any step can be skipped.** The walk is the routine, not completing every action. If he skips WATER and PICK today, that is fine -- he still did his garden morning.

- **If he finishes in 2 minutes, he is done.** If he wants to stay longer, that is his choice. Never push for more.

- **Never extend past 5 minutes** even if there is more to do. The routine is short and predictable -- that is what makes it sustainable.

- **Lamination tip:** Print on A4, laminate if possible for outdoor use. Hang near the back door or on the garden fence.

- **Danish cues are for you to say aloud** -- the child follows the emoji icons, not the text.
